About

Created as a CS50x final project, steelDesign is based on the Egyptian code of practice for steel structures design (AM2011) and M.U.S.T's (Misr university for science and technology) CE403 course, it aims to help students solve problem sets by providing a step-by-step solutions to several structural steel design problems, it's intended for academic use only.
